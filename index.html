Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, is a bustling metropolis with a rich history and culture. Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is a world-famous beach resort town located in the Chittagong Division. The beach is the longest in the world, stretching for over 120 kilometers. I can imagine that you were amazed by the vast expanse of sand and the crystal-clear waters. You must have had a lot of fun swimming, sunbathing, and exploring the local villages.
